    Rules
  • Scoring System:
    • Each correct answer awards a fixed number of points (e.g., 10 points per correct answer).
    • Incorrect answers give no points
  • Answer Submission:
    • Once an answer is submitted, it cannot be changed. Choose your answers carefully!
  • No Backtracking:
    • Users cannot go back to previous questions once they have moved to the next one.
  • Leaderboard:
    • A real-time leaderboard will display the top scorers based on total points accumulated across quizzes.
    • Scores are updated immediately after the quiz is completed.
  • Multiple Attempts:
    • Users can take quizzes multiple times, but only the highest score will be saved for the leaderboard.
  • User Authentication Required:
    • Only registered and logged-in users can participate in the quizzes and save their scores.
